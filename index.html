Last Updated: April 7, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal information when you interact with our services:

  • Personal Data: Name, email, phone number, company details
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited
  • Cookies: Session cookies, preference cookies, security cookies

2. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have:

  • The right to access, correct, or delete your data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us or email privacy@stratixai.com.

3. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have:

  • The right to know what personal data is collected
  • The right to delete personal data (with exceptions)
  • The right to opt-out of data sales
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To submit a CCPA request, use our contact form and select "Privacy Request".

4. Cookie Policy

We use cookies to:

  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze site traffic
  • Improve user experience

You can manage cookies in your browser settings or via our cookie banner.

5. Contact Us

For privacy requests or questions: Contact Form or email privacy@stratixai.com.
